Veality is an app where users can browse and purchase virtual reality experiences at a Veality location near them. Main purpose of the app, was to attract the senior adult users who are the fastest-growing group of smartphone users. By simplifying the functions, it would allow them to purchase them as gifts for their friends and family members without any hassles.

Role: I worked alone on this project. I first set out to research my users and the competition

User Research - Guerilla Testing

I chose to conduct my user research at the Malls and Coffeeshops due to these locals being a favorite daytime hangout for this particular demographic.

Competition and Market Analysis

The virtual reality market is projected to explode to 120 Billion Dollars by the year 2026. Most of the competitors were just a few start-ups.

Personas

I decided to add a second user, The Gamer. After my initial interviews, it was discovered that grandparents entertainment spending habits are ,most of time, influenced by their grandkids (video gamers, in this case!).

Storyboard

A story of a grandfather who has no idea what to buy his grandson for his birthday.

1st iteration and User Feedback

After conducting user testing on a low-fidelity mockup using InVision, based on the feedback from both users types, re-designed the screens.

How Might We

I came up with 3 categories of how might we make the app easier to use, cost-effective and create demand.

User Flow - Happy Path

Final iteration was designed on Adobe XD, keeping in mind a smooth user flow experience. Always focusing on the user reaching the end and making that final purchase!
